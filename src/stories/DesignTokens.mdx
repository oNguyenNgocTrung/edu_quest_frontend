## Spacing & Rounding

- Touch targets: minimum 44px
- Card corners: `rounded-xl` (12px), `rounded-2xl` (16px), `rounded-3xl` (24px)
- Button corners: `rounded-xl` (12px)
- Badge corners: `rounded-full`
- Standard gaps: `gap-2` (8px), `gap-3` (12px), `gap-4` (16px), `gap-6` (24px)
